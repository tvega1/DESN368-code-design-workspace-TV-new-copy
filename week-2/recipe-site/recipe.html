Brown Sugar Cinnamon Bars

About This Recipe

This warm and chewy dessert combines the rich flavor of brown sugar with the cozy spice of cinnamon. Perfect for fall gatherings or a comforting snack!

Inspired by traditional blondies, these bars are simple to make and use ingredients you likely already have in your pantry.

Chef’s Tip: For extra depth, toast your cinnamon for 30–60 seconds in a dry pan before mixing.

Ingredients

Fresh Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Pantry Staples

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, divided
  • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat and prep: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease or line an 8×8-inch baking pan with parchment.
  2. Make the batter: In a bowl, whisk melted butter and brown sugar. Add egg, honey, and vanilla; whisk until combined.
  3. Add dry ingredients: Stir in flour, salt, and ½ teaspoon cinnamon until just combined.
  4. Spread and top: Pour batter into pan. Mix granulated sugar and remaining ½ teaspoon cinnamon and sprinkle over batter.
  5. Bake: Bake 22–25 minutes until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely before cutting.

Notes & Variations

For extra texture, fold in ½ cup chopped toasted pecans or chocolate chips before baking.

Storage: These bars stay fresh up to 3 days in an airtight container.
