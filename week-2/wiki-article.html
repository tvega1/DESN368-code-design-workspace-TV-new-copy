LeBron James

Biography

LeBron James (born December 30, 1984) is an American professional basketball player widely regarded as one of the greatest athletes in the history of the NBA. Known for his exceptional athleticism, leadership, and longevity, he has played for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and currently, the Los Angeles Lakers.

Beyond basketball, LeBron is a philanthropist and entrepreneur, having opened the I PROMISE School in his hometown of Akron, Ohio, to support at-risk youth and their families.
