Serial Sex Offender Is Sought In Killing of Homeless Woman

Investigation continues in New York City

Authorities in New York City are searching for a serial sex offender suspected in
the recent killing of a homeless woman late last week. The victim, whose identity
has not yet been released, was found in a sleeping bag near an underpass on
the Lower East Side. Investigators believe the perpetrator may have committed
other similar crimes in recent months and are urging anyone with information to
contact the NYPD tip line.
