Lana Del Rey

Lana Del Rey, born Elizabeth Woolridge Grant, is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. Known for her cinematic style and melancholic themes, she blends vintage Americana with modern pop culture. Her music explores tragic romance, glamour, and melancholia.
